  • Warframe

    Warframe is a sci-fi looter/shooter about several factions vying for control over the various planets, and your goal is to stop them while uncovering their true nature, and your own.

    I've played this on and off for many years, seeing the game grow and evolve over time with the amazing dev team this game has. A last ditch effort to save a dying company, the game has recieved continuous updates since it's launch in 2012, and is now a huge success and the backbone of their brand.
